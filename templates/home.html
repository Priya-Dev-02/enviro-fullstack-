About Us

“Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world; indeed, it is the only thing that ever has.”

— Margaret Mead

Welcome to Enviro we are passionate advocates for a sustainable future, committed to transforming the landscape of waste management. With a focus on recycling e-waste, food waste, and household waste, we strive to redefine how societies approach their environmental footprint. Our journey is fueled by innovation, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to streamline sorting and recycling processes. Beyond the technological realm, we champion community involvement through awareness programs, fostering a collective commitment to responsible waste disposal. Enviro is not just a waste management initiative; it's a movement towards a cleaner, greener planet. Join us in shaping a more sustainable world for generations to come.

Services

Discover eco-friendly solutions at Enviro. We specialize in managing food waste, e-waste, and household waste. Transforming household waste into recycled products, we're committed to a greener future. Join us for responsible and sustainable waste management.

Organic Waste

Enviro collects organic waste from the
community,converting it into fertilizer for a sustainable and circular waste management solution

E Waste

Enviro collects organic waste from the community, transforming food scraps and garden clippings into nutrient-rich compost.
This initiative promotes responsible disposal and contributes to a sustainable circular economy.

Household Waste

Household waste, or domestic waste, includes materials discarded by individuals and families
at home, such as food scraps, paper, plastics, glass, metals, yard waste, and textiles.
